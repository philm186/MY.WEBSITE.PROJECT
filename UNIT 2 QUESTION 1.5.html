Describing copyright and other constraints affecting websites?

copyright protects your work and stops others from using it without your permission and protects your work of authorship, including text, graphics, photographs ,sound recordings and audio visual elements of websites and their underlying computer programs. It prevents people from copying your work and distributing copies of it, it even stops them from renting, lending, performing, showing or playing your work in public. they cant make adaptation of your work. other constraints affecting websites could be if your designing a website for a client and they don't find your design fit with their brand. What if your design takes a long time to develop? you may either miss the deadlines or just can’t meet the client’s expectations as a result of not considering the unique constraints of your design project. you need to know where and when to spend resources and energy in the project as per budgetary and other constraints.
